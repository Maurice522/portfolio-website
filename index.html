SPARK

Asteroid Capture has massive potential for research and development. And now with the discovery of Hydroxyl Groups on the Near-Earth Asteroid Bennu by NASA, we thought of SPARK or Space Perturbation Asteroid Recovery Kernel. A research study conducted to find a feasible way to capture, transport and recover asteroids so that they can be researched upon at the International Space Station. We calculated the trajectory paths, damping velocities, impacter collision energy and methods to transfer it from orbit.

Lingz

There are more than ten thousand massive open online courses(MOOCs). However they are present in only one language - English. English is a language spoken only by 20% of the World’s population. This is where Lingz comes into play: Lingz is a service that translates Massive Online Open Courses(MOOCs) and other video tutorials to vernacular languages. Lingz will help provide people with a new way to translate education and go beyond the boundaries of the English language. Lingz qualified for the Semi-Finals of Conrad Innovation Challenge 2018-19.

ARTIFEX - Art in Space

When Mr. Yusaku Maezawa reaches space with his team of artists to attempt what has never been attempted before, to create art, art that has the power to unite the world, he will need a tool, a tool that will enable him to thwart the problem that space throws towards him, microgravity and create stunning pieces of art. Our project aims to enable artists to produce artworks in space using the vast domains of science and technology. In this project, we aim to find a solution as to how to paint, utilizing all possibilities of space.
